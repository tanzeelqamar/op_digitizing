CAP SCAN

Cap digitizing requires careful planning due to curved surfaces and tight spaces — and that’s where we excel. We optimize your designs specifically for caps to ensure minimal thread breaks, sharp details, and smooth stitching across all types of headwear. Whether it’s a structured 6-panel cap, snapback, or beanie, our cap digitizing service ensures your logo or artwork is perfectly aligned and stitch-optimized. Ideal for sports teams, fashion brands, and promotional giveaways.

PUFF DIGITIZING

Puff digitizing is the art of creating raised embroidery using foam under the stitches — and we do it right. Our 3D puff files are carefully crafted with the right density, underlay, and stitch sequencing to bring depth and dimension to your caps, jackets, and more. Puff embroidery requires unique digitizing techniques like capping, overlapping, and removing extra stitches to prevent needle breaks. We provide tested, machine-ready 3D puff files with optimized paths for flawless results — every time

SLEEVES DIGITIZING

Sleeve digitizing requires compact, precise design placement for narrow embroidery areas. We create stitch files that are perfectly optimized for sleeve sizes, ensuring clean detailing, smooth runs, and high-quality output on every garment. Embroidering on sleeves? We’ve got you covered. Our sleeves digitizing service ensures your designs fit perfectly in tight areas without distortion or thread issues — whether it’s for branding, initials, or decorative elements.

APPLIQUE DIGITIZING

Looking to reduce stitch density and still make a bold impact? Our applique digitizing service is the perfect choice. Whether it’s for logos, letters, or large artwork, we deliver files that align perfectly with fabric patches, giving your designs texture, dimension, and durability.
